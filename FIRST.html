Achivements

  • 1991-Created the Linux Kernel and the version control system Git.
  • 2004-Named one of the "100 Most Influential People in the World" by Time magazine in 2004
  • 2010Received an honoracy doctrate from the University of Helsinki in 2010.
  • 2010-Received the 2010 Millennium Technology Prize and the 2012 Free Software Foundation's Award for the Advancement of Free Software.
  • 2013-Inducted into the Internet Hall of Frame in 2013 for his contributions to the development of the Linux Kernel and the creation of Git.
  • 2014-Inducted into the Hall of Frame of the Computer History Muemum in 2014
  • 2015-Received the 2015 IEEE Computer Society Computer Pioneer Award
  • 2019-Received the 2019 ACM SIGOPS Hall of frame Award
  • 2021-Awarded the Takeda Foundation's 2021 Takeda Award for Social Contribution,which recognizes individuals who have made significant to society through technology
  • 2021-Received the 2021 IEEE Computer Society Computer Pioneer Award
